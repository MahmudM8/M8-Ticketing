About M8-Ticketing

Our Story

Founded in 2023, M8-Ticketing was born out of a passion for live events and a frustration with the current ticketing landscape. We saw an opportunity to create a platform that puts both event-goers and organizers first.

Our journey began in Lagos, Nigeria, where we hosted our first major concert with just 500 attendees. Today, we've grown to serve thousands of events across multiple countries.

What sets us apart is our commitment to transparency, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
