Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase tickets?

To purchase tickets, browse our events, select the one you want, choose your tickets, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive your tickets via email immediately after payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money payments.

Can I get a refund for my tickets?

Refund policies vary by event. Check the event details for specific information. Generally, refunds must be requested at least 48 hours before the event.
