Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, purchase tickets, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Payment details (processed securely by our payment partners)
  • Event preferences and purchase history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process transactions and send ticket confirmations
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Send you relevant event recommendations (you can opt-out anytime)

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal information

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge complaints with data protection authorities

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through email or prominent notices on our service.
